 Currently in Year 2 of my course, Diploma in Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics, this course has given me the opportunity to not only pick up skills useful for the evolving economy, but also give back to my community and nation by applying these skills and knowledge.

 Fuelled by my desire to give back to the best of my ability, I took part in multiple community service projects and events through my CCA, Leo Club. I also helped organise events within my school by participating actively in my school's main interest group, ICT Society.
